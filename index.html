About AlterEgo Fitness

Based in Manchester England and founded in 2015 with just four stringers, AlterEgo Fitness is now a registered limited company with over 100 individual products and thousands of loyal customers from around the world. Our goal is pretty simple, to provide affordable quality gym wear without the price tag. Our Mission is simple, to become one of the BIGGEST and influential brands in the world. Our trustpilot rating is over 9 stars and we will forever continue to provide an easy, affordable but sleek service without compromise.
